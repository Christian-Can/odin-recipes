Lasagna

The Best Homemade Lasagna

This is one of my favorite lasagna recipes to make when I'm in the mood for something savory and cheesy!

Get a hold of your ingredients and follow these easy steps and you'll be sure to have yourself a wonderful and tasty dinner.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Cheeses
    • Ricotta
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
  • Tomato products
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
